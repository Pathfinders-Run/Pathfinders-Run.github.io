Who Are We

Pathfinders Run is a nonprofit organization dedicated to enhancing the physical, emotional, and social well-being of individuals who are blind or visually impaired through guided running and community support. We provide tailored programs, resources, and partnerships to ensure that running is accessible and enjoyable for all, regardless of visual ability.

Our Priorities

At Pathfinders Run, our mission is to empower individuals who are blind or visually impaired to embrace the joy and freedom of running. We strive to create an inclusive, supportive community where everyone can explore their potential, build confidence, and lead active, healthy lives. Through innovative programs, guided running opportunities, and partnerships with passionate volunteers, we aim to remove barriers and foster a sense of independence, achievement, and belonging. Together, we are paving the way for a future where vision is no limitation to movement and adventure.

The Intelligent Eye Application

It offers a valuable toolset for visually impaired individuals by providing voice cues that clearly indicate their current page. Using AI to recognize surroundings in real-time, it guides users through their environment with auditory feedback via a speaker.

The Intelligent Backpack Hardware

A light-weighted, cheap, and functional backpack that can detect the distances between the closest obstacles and feedback the user through different intensities of vibration.

The Intelligent Backpack Hardware - Front

The backpack features four laser distance sensors, each paired with a corresponding vibration motor, strategically positioned at chest and waist levels. As obstacles are detected, the vibration intensity increases proportionally to the user's proximity, providing intuitive spatial awareness beyond the range of a traditional cane.

The Intelligent Backpack Hardware - Back

The sensors and motors are connected to a multiplex, a microcontroller connected with a battery. They're all stored inside the backpack.
